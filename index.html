My process for perfection

1

Consultation

Getting to know you

Before I even begin working on your website, I must first understand your needs and motivations. In this phase, we will have in depth discussions about your site's aesthetics, functionality, budget and target audience to ensure the final site fits you perfectly.

I can also redesign existing websites to modernise, improve and add features.

By the end of this stage, we will have a clear vision about what the site will be, as well as an estimated final cost for the site.

2

Creation

Bringing It to Life

Once I have a solid idea about what you want done, I will get to work. I use modern technologies to ensure that the site looks professional, is secure and loads quickly.

Throughout development, I will consult you for feedback to ensure that the design is perfect for you.

3

Completion

Final Checks

After developing the site, I do one last round of feedback and testing to ensure perfection. I then deploy your site to the web so customers can access it.

After this stage, your site is good to go.

4

Continuation

Ongoing support

After your website is finished, I offer ongoing support. I can make changes to the sites appearance and functionality as well as add new content.
